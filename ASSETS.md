## Heads-up on the driver quotes

The three quotes are attributed to real F1 drivers (Hamilton, Verstappen,
Norris). Using real people's names + photos to imply endorsement of your team
can be legally iffy for sponsorship material. Consider either (a) clearly
framing them as general inspirational quotes, or (b) swapping to quotes from
your own team members / mentors. Your call — just flagging it.
